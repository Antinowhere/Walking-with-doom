Walking with Doom

Journey through the landscapes of your mind. Navigate between darkness and light, discover hidden truths, and uncover easter eggs along the way.

built by Liam Mason with Cursor, Sep 2025, SF

Welcome, Traveler

Controls:
Desktop: WASD to move, mouse to look, O to hide/show popups
Mobile: Use joystick to move, touch & drag to look

Purpose:
Walk between the dark and light sides of a divided room. As you venture deeper into each side, messages will appear reflecting that realm's nature.

Secrets:
Keep your eyes open for hidden easter eggs and special areas to discover!
